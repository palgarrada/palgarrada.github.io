Hi! I am a PhD Candidate at the Department of Economic History, Institutions, Policy and World Economy at the Universitat de Barcelona (UB), advised by Prof. Paloma Fernández Pérez.

I am also a member of the Centre d'Estudis Jordi Nadal and part of the research project "MEDCAP-INDERE," which focuses on business dynamics and resilience in the North-Western Mediterranean.

Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of Urban Economic History and Business History, with a primary focus on the transformation of major European cities. I specialize in analyzing public-private partnerships (PPP) and the role of mixed agencies in shaping urban development, using Barcelona (1985-2024) as a key case study.

Through a long-term historical perspective, my work investigates how these collaborations influence gentrification processes, economic resilience, and the evolution of urban districts like Ciutat Vella.
